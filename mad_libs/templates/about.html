About Mad Libs

"Mad Libs" is a word game invented by Leonard Stern and Roger Price, who published the first Mad Libs book in 1958. A Mad Libs book contains short stories or narratives with certain key words blanked out. One player prompts the other player for words to fill in the blanks in the story ("give me an adjective" or "name a male celebrity"). The first player then reads the often nonsenical and hilarious story back to the second player.
I created this app in 2014 as a small project to practice building web applications using Flask. I loved Mad Libs games as a child, and I wanted an opportunity to work with the Python natural language processing toolkit.
I hope you will take a few minutes to play the game, and that it might bring a smile and a laugh into your day.
